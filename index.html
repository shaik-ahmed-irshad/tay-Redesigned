About Us
Tay Healthcare:

Tay HealthCare is a health care management firm, which offers consultancy services to individuals and organizations.

We help individuals and organizations to register their healthcare license in UAE which allows them to work or practice their job here in UAE.

We are also the official representatives of the Ministry of Health UAE. We have a dedicated team that works round the clock to help you with your license application.

We have a strong network of medical professionals, which allows us to assess and get your license granted as soon as possible.

Our team is well-versed with the UAE healthcare regulations and policies.

We work closely with healthcare facilities, government bodies to deliver high quality services to the healthcare industry.
